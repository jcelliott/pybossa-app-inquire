Question Classification Tutorial

Hi! Thanks for helping out with this project. The next page will quickly explain what you have to do, and then you can get started.

The task is pretty simple. It will show you a question, for example:

How fast must a spacecraft travel to escape Earth's gravity?

You just have to decide what type of question it is. You select a category and then a list of sub-categories will appear. Select the sub-category and then click Submit. That's it!

So for our example you would choose "Numeric" for the category because the question is asking about some type of number, and then choose "speed" for the sub-category because it's asking about a speed.

Some questions will be harder to categorize, and some may seem like they belong in multiple categories. That's fine, just make your best guess.
